Dr. Sanduk Ruit

The god of Eyesight

Here is the biography of Dr. Sanduk Ruit's life:

  • 1944 - Born in Olangchungola, a remote village in eastern Nepal
  • 1963 - Graduates from the University of Delhi with a degree in medicine
  • 1966 - Completes his ophthalmology training at the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) in New Delhi
  • 1970 - Returns to Nepal and begins working at the Tilganga Eye Centre in Kathmandu
  • 1976 - Meets Dr. Fred Hollows, an Australian ophthalmologist, and begins a lifelong collaboration to combat blindness in Nepal
  • 1985 - Develops a low-cost, high-volume cataract surgery technique that can be performed in remote areas
  • 1992 - Establishes the Tilganga Institute of Ophthalmology, which becomes a leading eye care center in Nepal
  • 1994 - Begins conducting eye camps in rural Nepal, providing free cataract surgeries to thousands of people
  • 1995 - Receives the Ramon Magsaysay Award for International Understanding for his work in reducing blindness in Nepal
  • 2001 - Performs cataract surgery on the Dalai Lama, who had been suffering from cataracts
  • 2003 - Establishes the Himalayan Cataract Project, a non-profit organization dedicated to eradicating blindness in the Himalayan region
  • 2006 - Receives the Asia Pacific Award for Outstanding Contributions to Ophthalmology
  • 2007 - Performs over 100,000 cataract surgeries, with a success rate of over 95%
  • 2015 - Receives the Order of the Rising Sun, Gold Rays with Rosette, from the Government of Japan for his contributions to ophthalmology
  • 2018 - Receives the Padma Shri, one of India's highest civilian honors, for his contributions to ophthalmology
  • 2020 - Continues to work at the Tilganga Institute of Ophthalmology, leading a team of ophthalmologists and surgeons in the fight against blindness in Nepal and beyond

"The whole objective, aim and my passion and love is to see there remain no people with unnecessary blindness in this part of the world. It is important that the people do receive equitable service and not that haves receive and have nots don't receive it. I want to make sure that everybody receives it." - Dr. Sanduk Ruit
